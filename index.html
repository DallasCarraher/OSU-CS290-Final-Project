SPOTLIGHT 5

  • #1 Artist of the Week - The Upbeats

    This week's #1 featured Track was "Alone" by The Upbeats. featuring Tasha Baxter. This track has superior ear-splitting bass but beautiful vocals. Beatport

  • #2 Artist of the Week - Au5

    This week's #2 featured track was none other than "Crystal Mathematics" by Au5. We approve of both the instrumental and vocal mixes as they are equally as awesome. The original mix with vocals by Shaz Sparks is as perfect if not better than the instrumental.
    Beatport

  • #3 Artist of the Week - Stereotronique

    This week's #3 Featured track was "Jack dat Body" by Dabin & Black Tiger Sex Machine. Although, more specifically we chose Stereotronique's remix for it's awesome grungy edm bass.
    Beatport

  • #4 Artist of the Week - Zomboy

    This week's #4 Featured track was "Here to Stay" by Zomboy, while featuring sick vocals by Lady Chann.
    Beatport

  • #5 Artist(s) of the Week - Alesso & One Republic

    This week's #5 Featured track was "If I lose myself"; a collaborative track made by Alesso and One Republic. Ryan Tedder's sweet vocals mixed with Alesso's party synths is a fantastic mix.
    Beatport
